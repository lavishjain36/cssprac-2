ABOUT US





We are a group of people bound by the same mission. Our mission is to combat global warming by forestation – one tree at a time! That is why we created the Arbo bracelet, a carefully designed and handmade bracelet made with care from materials that do not harm the nature. For each bracelet sold we will plant one tree!

We love what we do because the entire project was created within a group of friends. And alongside doing something we consider amazing, we are having loads of fun. Our goal is to plant 15,000 trees by the end of 2016. If we reach the goal, our founder and the rest of the team plan to get a nature inspired tattoo (neither have tattoos).
